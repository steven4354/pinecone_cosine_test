4 billion.
 Partner Adam Dinow led the Cooley team advising Thrasio.
 Silver Lake led the round, which included participation from existing investors Advent International, Upper90, Oaktree Capital Management, Peak6 and Corner Capital.
 Thrasio will use the new capital to continue acquiring promising brands – both domestically and internationally – while expanding distribution through additional channels.
